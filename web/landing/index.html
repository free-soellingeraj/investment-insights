How It Works

A 4-stage pipeline converts raw public documents into dollar-value estimates of each company's AI cost savings and revenue generation potential over a 3-year horizon.

1

Collect

Gather raw documents from public sources for each company in the index universe.

SEC 10-K & 10-Q filings
AI news & product launches
USPTO patent records
Job posting signals
2

Extract

Identify relevant AI evidence passages and classify each on two axes.

Target: cost, revenue, or general
Stage: planned, invested, or realized
Confidence score per passage
Keyword or LLM extraction
3

Value

Estimate the dollar impact of each evidence passage over a 3-year horizon.

BLS salary x AI applicability
Revenue penetration rates
Horizon shape by stage
Year 1 / Year 2 / Year 3 estimates
4

Score

Aggregate dollar estimates into company-level metrics and quadrant assignments.

Cost Opportunity ($)
Revenue Opportunity ($)
Cost & Revenue Capture ($)
Quadrant placement

Dollar Values, Not Scores

Every estimate is in real dollars: how much AI could save a company in costs, or how much new revenue AI could generate. A 3-year horizon shows the ramp from planned to realized value.

Evidence-Level Granularity

Every piece of evidence is classified by target (cost or revenue) and maturity stage (planned, invested, or realized). Each carries its own dollar estimate and horizon shape so you can see exactly what drives every number.

Public Data Only

Every estimate is grounded in public data: BLS workforce statistics, SEC filings, USPTO patents, and financial APIs. Full source citations on every evidence item. No black boxes.

METHODOLOGY. The AI Opportunity Index scores companies on a 4-value framework (cost/revenue x opportunity/capture) using publicly available data including SEC filings, BLS workforce statistics, USPTO patents, financial APIs, and other public sources. Every piece of evidence is classified by target dimension (cost, revenue, or general) and stage (planned, invested, realized). Scores are computed algorithmically and may not capture all relevant factors affecting a company's AI prospects. See our technical whitepaper for methodology details.
