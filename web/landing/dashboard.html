4-Value Scoring Framework

Every company is scored on four dimensions: Cost Opportunity (AI's potential to reduce internal costs), Cost Capture (actual AI deployment for cost savings), Revenue Opportunity (AI's potential to grow revenue), and Revenue Capture (actual AI products and revenue-facing investment). General AI announcements are tracked separately and do not inflate capture scores. Read the whitepaper
